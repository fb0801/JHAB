Why would you use Content Managemt Systems (CMS)?

By Farhan 2019

The online world is always changing and many companies of a small, medium or large size or even individuals are attempting to make the leap into the new world of technology. Behind almost every company online is a website and with technical skills becoming more requested by people and most professional software to develop websites costing alot of money to create and host the website, companies and individuals are now looking into content management systems.
But Farhan why would you use Content Management Systems instead of hiring a web desinger and other programmers for the website?
The answer is simple Gon its cheaper and easier to use.

The reason why is instead of hiring mulitple designers and backend programmers, you can instead use CMS sites/software like Adobe muse, wix.com, WordPress just to name a few to help build your site by dragging and dropping items into different places on your website without having to do any HTML,CSS, JavaScript, PHP or anyothers used for web development as these applications make it easier to make pages and does not require you to have any programming skills or experience. Some of these CMS otherwise known as website builders also offer a cheap price to make your website avalible online so people can view it which makes it easier on you not having to create a server to host your site from.

As well as;

Can easily carry out website maintenance
They furnish SEO-friendly URLs.
Make integrated and online assistance such as discussion boards available to users.
They facilitate group-based permission systems.
Provide complete template support as well as customisable templates.
Have easy wizard based install and version procedures.
Provide admin panel with multiple language support.
They dictate minimal server requirements.
They avail the users of integrated file managers.
